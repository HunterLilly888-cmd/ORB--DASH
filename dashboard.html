How to use VWAP
↗ Above VWAP
Long bias only. Look for OR High breakouts with volume confirmation.
↘ Below VWAP
Short bias only. Look for OR Low breakdowns with volume confirmation.
⚠ At VWAP rule
If price is within 2–3 pts of VWAP — do not enter. VWAP acts as a magnet and will pull price back. Wait for a clear break and hold above or below before entering.
